The chosen colors for the app are white, dark blue and light blue. Blue is the color of the ocean and the sky which fits with the theme of aerial and ocean travel. It can often symbolize stability, competence and reliability. It was important for me to reinfroce these emotions so that the consumer would be confident in using this product while traveling worry free.
The chosen typeface for the app is Raleway.
Raleway has an elegant feel to it, which goes well the overall design of the app. The typeface has a large variety of styles and because of that it's easy on the eyes.

"SmarTravel" is an app which synchronises with your "SmarTravel" suitcase. The app helps you find the exact location of your suitcase during your travels. Apart from indicating your suitcase's location the app offers additional features like-indicating the suitcase's weight, provide you with built in wi-fi and charge your phone.
