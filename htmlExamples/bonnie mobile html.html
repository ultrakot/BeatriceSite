The chosen color scheme for the site is a variety of bright colors. The purpose is to let the bright colours to catch the eye of the potential customer and to make him realise the type of clothing that the site is selling.
The chosen typeface for the site is Ploni.
Ploni is a precise and geometric typeface that goes well with the geometric
shapes that go along the whole site. Also, the typeface doesn't overshadow the overall design.
