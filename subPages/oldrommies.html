About The Project
"Roomies" is an app which helps you find the perfect roomate. The process of finding a suitable roomate be arduous and time cosuming. "Roomies" was created to tackle these issues by being able to carefully filter out candidates.The goal of the design is to be clean, young, modern and most importantly - user friendly.

The chosen color scheme for the app is a variety of purple shades. Purple is expressive, creative and embracing – which is important in a modern and young society.
I wanted to give the app a trendy yet reliable feel that will make my target audiance - young people, to trust the app that it will help them. Purple always draws attention and charms the eye of the user.

Icons
The icons were styled to match the illustrations that are displayed along the app. The characteristics are thin lines, a minimalistic look with occasional gaps between the lines for a modern design.
