The chosen typeface for the headlines is Quicksand.
Quicksand is a display sans serif with rounded terminals. It is designed for display purposes, that's why I chose to use it for headlines only. Also, the roundness of the typeface isn't stressful on the eyes and gives an aspect of nature.

The chosen typeface for the site is Segoe UI.
Segoe UI is friendly, easy to read and understand. It doesn't take the attention from the design and gives a comfortable reading experience to the potential viewer.

The chosen colors for the site are shades of green and yellow. Green was chosen to represent flora, the natural world and tranquility. The colors yellow and orange were chosen to represent the sun and the energy of life.
Together the whole color scheme tells us the purpose of the app- to connect us to our plants and give them
energy and life.
