The chosen color scheme for the site is a variety of bright colors. The design language was already established within the studio. The goal was to stick to that language and showcase the work in an interesting way.
The chosen typeface for the site is Almoni.
Almoni is a precise, neutral bilingual typeface that fits the overall design of the landing page. It lets the design to be the center of attention and doesn't overshadow it.
